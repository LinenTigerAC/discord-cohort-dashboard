Cohort Overlap

Are these cohorts different groups of people, or the same people wearing different labels? Each row shows what share of that cohort's members also belong to the cohort in each column — based on whether they ever held that role across the full dataset, not just the latest week. Read it row-first: "of everyone in the row cohort, X% are also in the column cohort."

The Ladder

Raw population at each rung of the leveling system — Gorilla through T-Rex — cumulative, latest week vs. quarter start.
06

Activation

Activated = sent 3+ messages in their first week in the data. This mirrors Discord's own first-day activation benchmark, but measured weekly rather than daily since that's the grain we have — treat it as a related, not identical, number. Also: this can only measure people who sent at least one message, so it's an upper bound on true server-wide activation, not a measure against your full ~430k membership.
